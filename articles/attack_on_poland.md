## Attack on Poland

The attack on Poland, also known as the Invasion of Poland, was a military campaign that marked the beginning of World War II. On September 1, 1939, Germany launched a surprise attack on Poland, utilizing a strategy known as Blitzkrieg, or "lightning war." This involved rapid and coordinated strikes by air and ground forces to quickly overwhelm the Polish defenses.
